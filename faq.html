QUESTIONS AND ANSWERS

Useful Information

Here are some of the most commonly asked questions we’ve received from our customers. If you don’t find the answer you’re looking for, please reach out and we will get back to you shortly.

DO YOU MAKE ANY VEGAN ITEMS?

Absolutely! Our bakery proudly offers a variety of delectable vegan treats, from cakes to cookies and more. We believe in catering to diverse dietary preferences and ensuring everyone can enjoy our sweet creations.


DO YOU OFFER GLUTEN FREE ITEMS?

Yes, we have a range of gluten-free options available to cater to various dietary needs. Our goal is to ensure that all our customers can savor our delectable treats, regardless of dietary restrictions.


HOW DO I KEEP MY PURCHASES FRESH?

To keep your purchases fresh, we recommend storing them in an airtight container or wrapping them tightly to prevent air exposure. For cakes, refrigeration is often best, while cookies and cupcakes can be stored at room temperature. Enjoy them within the recommended time frame to savor their freshness.
